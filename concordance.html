How to play
A 30-second walkthrough
A dictionary entry describes its headword — the word being defined — through clues: definitions, synonyms, antonyms, examples. In Glossari, the headword is missing. Read the clue, then assemble the headword from three word segments in the right order.

Word Segments
Each puzzle has four entries and twelve word segments. The mechanic stays the same — read the clue, identify the headword, assemble its three segments in the right order.
