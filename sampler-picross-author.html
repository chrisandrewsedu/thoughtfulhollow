How to paint: select a shape, then click or drag across cells to place them. Select a fabric colour (and optionally a pattern) and click a region to paint it (drag to fill many cells). Pattern is purely decorative — the puzzle only tests colour. The rotate tool cycles a placed piece when clicked (triangles and bars have 2 orientations, curves have 4; squares and quarter-square triangles don't rotate). The eraser clears a cell. Cmd/Ctrl-Z to undo.
